About

Excursion is a global personal travel guide for the brave hearted. It is created to help you navigate, discover, share and enjoy your travels with an easy-to use discovery app that is created for your convenience.

The developer of Excursion, Dart Vader came up with the idea of a global database to navigate local areas in 2013. The company expanded its revenue exponentially in 2 years and become worlds #1 leading travel dictionary that is used in 100 countries in 100 languages.
